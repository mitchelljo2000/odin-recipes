Rice

Description

Rice is bland? Boring? Tasteless? These are the words of someone who's never tasted perfectly cooked rice.

Try this recipe and I guarantee it'll leave you wanting more. This recipe is perfect for week night meals or meal prep.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water

Steps

  1. Gather your rice and water with a 2:3 rice water ratio
  2. Bring the water to a boil
  3. Add the rice to the boiling water than turn the temperature down to let it simmer for 15 minutes
  4. Remove pot from heat and let it sit for 10 minutes
  5. Fluff rice with a fork then serve
