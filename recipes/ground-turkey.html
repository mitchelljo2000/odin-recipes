Ground Turkey

Description

Ground turkey is bland? Boring? Tasteless? Nonsense! These are the words of someone who's never tasted perfectly cooked ground turkey.

Try this ground turkey recipe and I guarantee it'll leave you wanting more. This recipe is perfect for week night meals or meal prep.

Ingredients

  • Ground turkey
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Flatten ground turkey on pan set on stove with high heat
  2. Let one side brown as you sprinkle with salt and pepper
  3. After about 4 minutes, flip the turkey then break into pieces
  4. Stir
  5. When no longer pink, remove from pan
